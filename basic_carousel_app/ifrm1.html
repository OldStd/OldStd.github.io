I come across the "web components" when I search for alternatives for iframe. Some people had developed some packages that we can just adapt and use.

Here, I make use of one package called "basic-carousel" in the middle module.

This application acts like a slide-show to display a series of pictures by either clicking the left or right arrows or clicking on the buttons at the bottom.

I know, it is a little distraction from the regular class.

Just for fun.
