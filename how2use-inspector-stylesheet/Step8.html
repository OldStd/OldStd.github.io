To complete the action

  1. Choose "background-color" and press Enter
  2. Type "cor" and again a whole list of colors are dropped down
    • Choose "coral" and press Enter
    • We see the background-color changed to coral
    • on the web-page

We can now update our stylesheet "ss-container.css" as follows:

div#container {
    width: 912px;
    height: 912px;
    border: 2px solid red;
    background-color: coral;
}
